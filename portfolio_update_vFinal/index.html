About Me

My name is Brett Jones. I am an actor, costume designer, teaching artist, administrator, and hopeful web designer. I live in the Willow Mansion, a beautiful West Oakland Victorian house.

I have been teaching and acting for more than 8 years. I've been a lead teaching artist and Conservatory Coordinator for the California Shakespeare Theatre for the last 3 years. Being present and adaptable is the cornerstone of good theatre. While trying to be present and adapt to technology in the Bay Area, I have started to study Front End web design as a way to continue to understand the Technological landscape of the city I live, and also to bring my skill to the artform I love.
